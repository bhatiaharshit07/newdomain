## Abstract

Agentic AI will not be adopted in enterprises because agents are clever. It will be adopted when systems are reliable, governable, observable, and useful to real workflows.

## Topics

- Agent architecture
- Memory and context
- Tool access
- Human approval
- Observability
- Governance

## Audience

Technology leaders, product teams, architects, and engineering leaders building production AI platforms.
