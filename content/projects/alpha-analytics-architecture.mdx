## Product Context

Operational teams need signals they can trust. A computer vision platform becomes valuable when detections become reliable business events.

## Architecture Notes

- Edge inference captures visual state.
- Event pipelines normalize detections.
- Analytics layers convert events into operational signals.
- Dashboards support repeated decisions.

## Tradeoffs

Real-time systems need careful balance between accuracy, latency, and noise reduction.
